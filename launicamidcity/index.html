De Boyle Heights a Mid-City

Same family, same pots, new neighborhood.

La Unica started in 2017 as a Boyle Heights original, earning its name one quesataco at a time. When the TV crews came — Diners, Drive-Ins & Dives, Discover LA — they found what the neighborhood already knew: birria done with patience, tortillas done by hand, and consomé that doesn't cut corners.

The Mid-City outpost on Venice Blvd brings that same kitchen discipline west — so this side of town doesn't have to cross the river for the real thing.
