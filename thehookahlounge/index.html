Settle In

This is a lounge in the old sense of the word.

Step off Pico and the pace changes. Deep, comfortable couches. Ornate hookah vessels catching the light. Music tuned loud enough to feel and low enough to talk over. The Hookah Lounge carries the Middle Eastern lounge tradition the way it's meant to be — unhurried, generous, and centered on good company.

With more than 200 flavors behind the counter, there's a bowl for the purist, the sweet tooth, and the friend who says "surprise me." Take a couch inside under the warm glow, or claim a table on the outdoor patio when the LA evening cooperates — which is most of them.
