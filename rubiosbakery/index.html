Our Story

Two decades on Pico

For twenty years, Rubio's has been where Mid-City marks its milestones — the Saturday-morning concha run, the quinceañera cake pickup, the tres leches that shows up at every family table.

It's a family legacy with serious training behind it: our kitchen is led by a Le Cordon Bleu‑trained pastry chef who brings classical technique to the recipes this neighborhood grew up on.
